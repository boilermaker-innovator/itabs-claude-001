Think of it like a council that has to vote before anything happens. No single member can act alone.

How It Works

Consult-Before-Execute protocol
‚ñº
Why
The Process
Help

The key innovation is CBE - Consult Before Execute. No AI action happens without multi-perspective verification first.

Before Any AI Output:

  • All four roles must be consulted
  • Drift detection checks for weird behaviour
  • Ethical arbitration resolves conflicts
  • Trust Score calculated (combines fairness, logic, uncertainty)
  • Audit trail created automatically

MASI Trust Score (Tmasi) - A number that tells you how confident the system is in its decision. Transparent and verifiable.

The trust score formula and failure-containment logic are detailed in the technical specifications.

Governance Model

How it stays accountable
‚ñº
Why
Structure
Help

MASI isn't just tech - it includes a governance structure to keep everything accountable and incentivise good behaviour.

Two Key Components:

  • MASI DAO: Decentralised governance - no single company controls it
  • Modular Contribution Credits: Rewards for building safe, interoperable modules

The goal: an open ecosystem where safety is baked in, not bolted on.

DAO = Decentralised Autonomous Organisation. Think of it like a co-op where rules are enforced by code.
